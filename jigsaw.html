Project Description

Our project is an application that empowers users to explore, discover, and share information about their communities. In doing so, our app enhances the experience of walking around your neighborhood by transforming it into an immersive experience, highlighting things you may not have known or seen. This app will allow users to explore their neighborhoods in new ways and partner physical activity and mental stimulation to improve wellness. Additionally, our product promotes community wellness by offering a way for residents to learn about or contribute to their neighborhoodâ€™s history and culture. As neighborhoods change, this app will give users a way to document history. The platform allows users to share their own experiences and community knowledge, and to connect with other residents with similar interests and curiosities.

Design Question

How can we utilize technology to encourage neighborhood exploration and community connection during quarantine?
